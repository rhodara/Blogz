These are my Secret Moves ::

*My Thunder Attack*

#1. Let the ball go to the guy either at the top or bottom.

#2. Then let the guy (either at the top or bottom) pass the ball QUICKLY to the guy in the middle! #3. Then just "POUND IT" (The ball, of course!) with your middle guy!

*My Super Corner Kick*

#1. Let the ball goes to the guy either at the top or bottom.


#2. Then let the guy either at the top or bottom kick the ball with the edge of their foot.

p.s. Depends on side: If the ball is at the top, kick it with the right edge of the foot; If the ball is at the bottom, kick it with the left edge of the foot.
